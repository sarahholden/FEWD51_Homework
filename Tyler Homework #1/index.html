About Me

As a first and second grade Spanish Bilingual classroom teacher in San Francisco, I developed a passion for innovative instructional strategies that lead students from all backgrounds to success. Later, as an Instructional Reform Facilitator at a dynamic elementary school in San Francisco's Mission District, I worked to align systems and structures to support teacher development and growth. Now, as an Assistant Principal at the same school, I look for opportunities to streamline and improve tools and systems to put teachers in the best position to design and deliver engaging, joyful instruction to their students.

I am looking to learn more about the intersection of technology and learning for teachers and professionals. In particular, I am passionate about developing teacher-directed tools for professional learning and development.

Feel free to reach out to me (tylerpwoods@gmail.com) or connect through social media if you share similar passions or interests!
